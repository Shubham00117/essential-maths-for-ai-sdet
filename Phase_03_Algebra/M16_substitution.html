What is Substitution?

Substitution means replacing a variable with a number in an expression or equation.

It's like playing a game of "swap" - you take out the letter and put in the number!

Also called: "Evaluating an expression" or "Finding the value"

Simple Analogy:

üéÅ Gift Box Analogy

Your expression: x + 5

The variable x is like an empty gift box üì¶

When we substitute, we put a number into the box:

Now the expression becomes: 3 + 5 = 8
‚ú® SUBSTITUTION IN SIMPLE WORDS:
1. Look at the variable (letter)
2. See what number it equals
3. Replace the letter with that number
4. Calculate the answer
