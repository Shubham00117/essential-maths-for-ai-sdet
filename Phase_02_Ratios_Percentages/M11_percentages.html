What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way to express a number as a fraction of 100.

The symbol % means "per hundred" or "out of 100"

Think of it as: "A part out of 100"

Real-World Examples:

üìä Test Score

You scored 85 out of 100

That's 85%

(85 per 100)
üí∞ Discount

A shirt costs $100
You get 20% off

That's $20 discount

(20 per 100)

75% = 75/100 = 3/4
